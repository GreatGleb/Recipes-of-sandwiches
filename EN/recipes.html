Eight hot sandwiches

Hot sandwiches - this is a real find for those who do not have time to prepare a full meal, but I really want to eat. In the review - a few simple and delicious recipes.

1. Sandwich
    "Caprese"

Ingredients: bread, mozzarella, tomatoes, lettuce, basil, olive oil.

Cooking time: 10 min.

Calorie: 111 kcal.

2. Sandwich with
     ham and cheese

Ingredients: bread, cheese, tomatoes, ham, salt, ground pepper.

Cooking time: 10 min.

Calorie: 144 kcal.

3. Chicken Sandwich
     with Bacon and Avocado

Ingredients: bread, avocado, chicken, bacon, lettuce, tomato, lemon juice.

Cooking time: 30 min.

Calorie: 186 kcal.

4. Egg and Spinach
     Sandwich

Ingredients: bread, spinach, scallions, eggs, olive oil.

Cooking time: 20 min.

Calorie: 136 kcal.

5. Mexican
     burger

Ingredients: bun, mayonnaise, ground beef, paprika, green onion, tomato, lettuce, garlic, salsa sauce.

Cooking time: 20 min.

Calorie: 136 kcal.

6. Club
     sandwich

Ingredients: bread, mayonnaise, cheese, bacon, chicken, tomato, onion, lettuce.

Cooking time: 25 min.

Calorie: 156 kcal.

7. Fast
Chicken Pie

Ingredients: flour, chicken, mayonnaise, sour cream, eggs, onion, lemon juice.

Cooking time: 1 hour 10 minutes.

Calorie: 163 kcal.

8. Sandwich with cheese,
tomatoes and smoked salmon

Ingredients: bread, smoked salmon, tomato, cheese, onion, olive oil.

Cooking time: 15 min.

Calorie: 136 kcal.
