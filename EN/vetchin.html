Sandwich with ham and cheese



The classic combination of ham and cheese is especially good in hot sandwiches. To make this sandwich, you will need 2 slices of brown bread, cheese, 2 slices of tomato, 2 slices of ham, salt and black pepper.


Place grated cheese on one slice of bread, place the ham and tomatoes on top, then sprinkle with plenty of grated cheese again and cover with the second slice of bread. Pepper and salt. Put in the microwave and wait until all the cheese has melted.

Ingredients

1h 10m 4 portions 163 kcal
bread
tomato
cheese
ham
spices
