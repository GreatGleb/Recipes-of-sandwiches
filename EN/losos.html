Sandwich with cheese, tomatoes and smoked salmon



The easiest and fastest option for those who have very little free time. Take two slices of whole grain bread. Lubricate them with olive oil, grate enough cheese on top.


Spread sliced tomato and smoked salmon on top. Again, rub the cheese. The final ingredient is finely chopped onion. We place the sandwich in the microwave until the bread is browned and the cheese is melted. Delicious and healthy snack ready.

Ingredients

15 m 4 portions 136 kcal
bread
salmon
cheese
tomato
onion
